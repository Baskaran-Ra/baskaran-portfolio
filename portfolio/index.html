01 · About

I build interfaces people don't have to think about.

I'm a Front-End Developer with 4+ years of experience building responsive, user-centric web applications — currently at PFEC Global India, where I led the migration of 18 country websites from WordPress to a decoupled React.js + Strapi architecture.

I care about the details that make a site feel fast and trustworthy: clean semantic markup, accessible components, and performance budgets that actually get respected. My stack centers on React, Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS, backed by real production experience with REST APIs, Redux Toolkit, and headless CMS platforms.

Outside the browser, I like getting my hands dirty with hardware — PCB diagnosis and repair — and I manage the web infrastructure for a small engineering firm on the side.
